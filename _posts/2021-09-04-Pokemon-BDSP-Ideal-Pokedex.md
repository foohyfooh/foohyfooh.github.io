This is the my idea for what I would like to see in the Pokémon Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl Pokédex based on the one from Pokémon Platinum. <br/>
The changes I made are
- I added Mr. Rime and Sylveon since they are evolutions of existing Pokémon in this Pokédex. These are similar to the addition of Gallade in Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire.
- I added Stantler since it is getting an evolution, Wyrdeer, in Pokemon Legends: Arceus.
- I moved the Gible line to directly before the legendary Pokémon to be closer to how the other Pokédexes list the pseudo-legendary Pokémon before the legendary Pokémon.
-  I added the legendary Pokémon that weren't in the Pokédex to it and moved all the legendary Pokémon together at the end.
- I moved Darkrai out of the mythicals section to be next its counter part Cresselia.
- I moved Phione after Manaphy since it should be a mythical given that you need to breed Manaphy to get it.
